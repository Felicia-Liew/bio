Kenya Hara


Art Director of MUJI


Okayama, Japan, 1958


Kenya Hara is a Japanese designer who works in multiple areas of design, including: graphic design, industrial design, branding and curating. He has an active voice among the design community through his advocation for new design practice. He also gained more widespread international attention in recent years for his public activities.
